About Pickles

Excessively open tabs consume a lot of memory.

Use 'Pickles' to pickle that excessively opened tabs.

'Pickles' is a simple Google Chrome extension.

You can install it from the link below(it will be redirected to Google web store).

Install now

How to instatll

How to Pickle.

  1. Click to Pickles icon.
  2. Click to SAVE button.
  3. Please enter Pickles name.
  4. Click to SAVE button.
  5. Pickle will be stored in your browser.

How to uninstatll

How to check Pickle.

  1. Click to Pickles icon.
  2. Click to tabs button.
  3. Click to Pickle to open tabs.
  4. Click to INFO button to check the Pickle.
  5. Click to DEL button to delete the Pickle.
